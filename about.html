Hi I'm Dave

Thanks for visiting Komi

I started sewing in 2020 making my own backpacking gear and experimenting with high-tech fabrics balancing strength and weight. Carrying these over into everyday use, I began making bags to be enjoyed anywhere. Currently, my bags consist of 1000d Cordura on the exterior, and ripstop nylon or X-Pac on the interior. Cordura is used in many military applications, and X-Pac originates from racing sail cloth. I sew each of these bags myself right here in South East Portland. They are water-repellent and should stand up to a lifetime of use. I hope you enjoy using them as much as I do making them.
